Projects Blog

Check out and contribute to our A Guide to Digital Transformation blog.

In there you will find the following 4 projects we are currently writing up, which address all the issues raised in the approach section, covering the key considerations and full technical solutions.

  • Building a Digital Presence - Create and host a website complete with user analytics and digital marketing campaign to accelerate growth and develop customer intimacy
  • Customer Relationship Management - Onboard a CRM tool to better manage interactions with customers and potential customers to improve customer service, and increase profitability.
  • Designing your data strategy - Design and develop bespoke web application to securely collect, store, analyse and present data
  • Driving Digital Transformation Through Data and AI - Using machine learning and statistical modelling to derive valuable insight and predictive capabilities that take your business to the next level
