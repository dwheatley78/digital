Approach

My aim is to support local businesses identify and deliver affordable high impact results through the effective adoption of digital technologies. To achieve this I address 3 key questions:

  • What can Data Analytics do for me?
  • Where do I start?
  • How to maximise business value?

In the community section of this site I provide a private discussion forum and details of free workshops we will be holding all with the goal of creating a network of businesses with whom we can share challenges, ideas and discoveries. Through working together we can open up a digital world of possibilities.

Alternatively feel free to reach out directly using the contact links at the bottom of the page.

Data Analytics

Data analytics equips businesses with the ability to drive efficiency through automation, predict and respond to events before they happen or target interventions for maximum impact through observing correlations / trends and grouping behaviours

While these technologies may seem complex and expensive they need not be. The key is selecting the right problems to tackle and using the right open source tools to tools deliver value fast.

Where to start

As the saying goes "With great power comes great responsibility". Too often it is It's easy to get carried away with overly complex and costly solutions but the key to success is to start small. Adhering to the service design thinking methodology ensures that what you produce remains desirable to your customers while delivering real business value to you. As results are delivered the solutions can be scaled to meet your full ambition.

Maximising value

Digital projects rarely exist in isolation. To get the maximum value you have to understand how they transform the way you work or the service you are able to offer your customers. Through ensuring digital solutions are backed up by effective change management we can ensure the full value is realised, and that it sticks.
