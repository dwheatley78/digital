3 Steps to Digital Success

WHEATLEY.digital aim to support local businesses identify and deliver affordable, high impact results through the effective adoption of digital technologies.

  • Selecting the right projects
  • Delivering the capabilty
  • Maximising the value

Real world examples of implimenting these projects can be found in the ideas section. Alternatively get in touch to discuss what WHEATLEY.digital can do for you.

Selecting the right projects

The key to selecting the right projects is to have a clear understanding of what you hope to achieve and ensuring it aligns with your company strategy.

Having set a clear objective, adopting a service design approach will help us identify where to start. Engaging users early, starting small and growing fast to deliver maximum value!

Example business objectives include:

  • Enhanced digital presence with supporting marketing strategy
  • Driving operational efficiency through process automation
  • Developing customer intimacy through enhanced prediction and insight
  • Enabling data driven decision making and forecasting
  • Offering new services to increase customer value

Delivering the capabilty

Now the fun begins! We can start testing and developing a solution. Picking the right open source tools to ensure that what we build is affordable, supportable and able to scale as your ambitions grow.

Example solutions include:

  • Building statistical models to predict future outcomes
  • Using machine learning to discover relationships and dependencies to enable better data driven decision making
  • Using natural language processing to provide real time analysis of social media and customer reviews
  • Deploying web applications to provide new and improved services to your customers
  • Monitor user behaviour and usage statistics to enable more targeted interventions
  • Developing a company data warehouse to securely store and retrieve valuable information

Maximising the value

Regardless of whether the objective of your digital transformation is to drive internal efficiency or improve customer satisfaction, involving the wider stakeholders throughout the process and obtaining early feedback is essential.

Once implemented ensuring digital solutions are backed up by effective change management and marketing will maximise the benefits and help embed the necessary changes into your company culture.

  • Change management and governance - embedding your new tools into your way of working.
  • Digital marketing - Growing your customer base and developing a better understanding of them.
  • Performance monitoring and feedback - Measure the effectivity of your solutions and refine.
